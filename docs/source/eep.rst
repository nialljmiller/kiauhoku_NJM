EEPs - Equivalent Evolutionary Phases
=====================================

This page will contain a discussion on the philosophy of EEPs used by ``kiauhoku`` and other codes and model grids. It is based on `MESA Isochrones and Stellar Tracks (MIST) 0: Methods for the Construction of Stellar Isochrones <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2016ApJS..222....8D/abstract>`_ by Aaron Dotter (2016). It will also provide an overview of the default EEPs used by ``kiauhoku`` as well as instructions on specifying your own custom EEPs.
